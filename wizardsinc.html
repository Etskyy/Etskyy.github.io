About

Project overview & goals

VR experience focused on believable hand interactions and playful systems. Built with Unity 6 and XR Interaction Toolkit. Features: grab/interact physics, gesture-based spell casting, and reactive props.

Worked On

My contributions

  • Interaction systems (XR Origin, Near/Far interactor tweaks, haptics).
  • Spell mechanics: gesture recognition, cooldowns, and VFX hooks.
  • Tooling: custom inspectors, debug gizmos, and performance profiling.
  • Level toys: destructible props, chain reactions, and sandbox events.
